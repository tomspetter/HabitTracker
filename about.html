About HabitDot

Our Philosophy

HabitDot was created with a simple belief: habit tracking should be beautiful, minimal, and respect your privacy.

Inspired by GitHub's contribution graph, HabitDot provides a year-at-a-glance view of your habits with colorful circular dots. No complicated features, no distracting notifications—just a clean, focused tool to help you build better habits.

Open Source & Free

HabitDot is completely free and open source under the MIT license. You can:

  • Use it for free, forever
  • View and audit the source code
  • Self-host on your own server
  • Fork and modify for your own needs
  • Contribute improvements back to the project

Privacy & Security

Your data belongs to you. HabitDot implements industry-standard security practices:

  • Bcrypt password hashing - Your password is never stored in plain text
  • Server-side sessions - Secure authentication with automatic timeout
  • CSRF protection - Protected against cross-site attacks
  • Rate limiting - Brute force attack prevention
  • Data export - Download your data anytime in CSV or JSON format
  • Self-hosting option - Keep complete control of your data

Technology Stack

Frontend

  • Tailwind CSS

Backend
